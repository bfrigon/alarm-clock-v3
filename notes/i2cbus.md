### I2C Bus address list ###

Address | Device               | Part #
--------|----------------------|-------------------
0xD0    | RTC                  | DS3231MZ
0xB0    | Audio amplifier      | TPA2016D2
0x1B    | Touch sensor IC      | AT42QT1070
0x78    | OLED display         | ER-OLED1602-4W
0x29    | Ambient light sensor | TSL2591
