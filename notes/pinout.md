Motherboard pinout
==================


J1 : DC Jack
------------

Pin | Name
----|------------------------
1   | VIN
2   | GND
3   | GND



J10 : PSU board
---------------

Pin | Name    | Pin | Name
----|---------|-----|---------------------------
1   | VIN     | 2   | VIN
3   | +5V     | 4   | +5V
5   | GND     | 6   | GND



J3 : PSU status
-------------------------

Pin | Name
----|------------------------
1   | Low battery signal
2   | On battery



J9 : Daughter board (DB-A)
-----------------------

Pin | Name                    | Pin | Name
----|-------------------------|-----|---------------------------
1   | +5V                     | 2   | +5V
3   | GND                     | 4   | GND
5   | RGB pixels data (clock) | 6   | RGB pixels data (lamp)
7   | CCS (3.3v)              | 8   | RGB pixels shutdonw
9   | Codec reset (3.3v)      | 10  | DREQ
11  | MOSI (3.3v)             | 12  | SCLK (3.3v)
13  | MISO                    | 14  | XDCS (3.3v)
15  | DVDD (3.3v LDO)         | 16  | Alarm switch



J3 : Daughter board (DB-B)
-------------------------

Pin | Name
----|------------------------
1   | SDA
2   | SCL
3   | Amplifier shutdown
4   | Keypad INT



J8 : Front panel
----------------

Pin | Name
----|------------------------
1   | +5V
2   | SDA
3   | SCL
4   | OLED reset
5   | ALS interrupt
6   | GND
